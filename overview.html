Project Overview

A visual narrative project about farewell and the act of moving forward.

Asym \ phase is built around farewell, growth, and self-reconciliation. It does not begin as a fully completed animated short, but as a visual development project: character design, poster design, model showcase, and the narrative structure that connects them.

What matters here is not spectacle, but restraint. The project stays with one emotional movement: looking back without remaining there, and continuing forward alone.

Title Structure

What “Asym \ phase” means

Asym

“Asym” comes from asymptote. It suggests something that keeps approaching without ever fully staying in place: memory, distance, and a feeling that can be carried forward but never held forever.

\

The backslash is not a separator. It is the central line of the project: a trajectory, a slanted path, a cut between phases, and the trace of the lantern’s arc through the dark. In a computing sense, it also suggests escape, path continuation, and entry into another layer. Here, it marks the moment when one stage can no longer be stayed in and another must begin.

Phase

“Phase” names a stage of life rather than a final conclusion. The project is not about closing everything completely. It is about carrying one phase into the next with a quieter, steadier understanding.

Current Form

Why the project is presented as a visual system

At its current stage, the project is presented through its most mature and controllable parts: character design, poster design, and model showcase. Rather than forcing a complete short film within a limited graduation timeline, this version concentrates on the visual language, symbolic structure, and emotional core of the work.

The model section uses AI-assisted high-detail display assets and Blender-based reconstruction to develop lighting, composition, and staged atmosphere. Full production tasks such as topology rebuilding, clothing separation, and rigging belong to the later stage, not to the current submission.
