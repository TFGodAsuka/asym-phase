Visual Narrative Project

A project built around farewell, transition, and the courage to continue forward alone.

Asym \ phase is a visual narrative project developed through poster design, character design, Blender-based spatial reconstruction, and a script-centered emotional structure.

The current version presents the project as a connected visual system rather than a fully completed animated short, focusing on the part of the work that is already most mature.

Title Structure

Why “Asym \ phase” is more than a title.

The title is the structural core of the project. It connects visual form, narrative logic, and emotional meaning through one compact system.

Asym

Asymptote

“Asym” comes from asymptote, suggesting something that keeps approaching without ever fully staying in place. It reflects memory, farewell, and emotional distance.

\

Trajectory / Transition

The backslash is not just a separator. It can be read as a path, a slanted trace, a dividing line, and the point where one phase gives way to another.

Phase

A stage of life

“Phase” keeps the project grounded in transition rather than closure. What matters is not a final ending, but how one stage is carried into the next.

Two Phases

Two connected emotional stages within one visual narrative.

Phase I · Premonition

Cold Unease

Companionship remains, but separation has already begun to tilt into the image.
Phase II · Farewell

Warm Restraint

After farewell, looking back no longer prevents moving forward.
