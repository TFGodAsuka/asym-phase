Return to the Child's View

We return to “my” point of view. The surrounding station noise returns faintly.

With dirty hands, “I” wipe away the tears and carefully open the letter.

Letter Close-up

Close-up: the contents of the letter.

At the same time, the adult man’s voice-over begins — warm, low, and full of strength, set against the child’s quiet sobbing.

Voice-over (V.O.) — Adult Man

Goodbye, brave child. Your adventure has only just begun. In the future, you will have to set out alone, again and again. But you must never be afraid. After all... you arrived in this world on your own.

（中文字幕：“再见了，勇敢的孩子，你的冒险才刚刚开始， 未来的你同样要学会独自出发，要说为什么的话， 毕竟，你本来就是独自来到这个世界上的啊。”）

Signature

Close-up: the signature at the end of the letter.

With love,
Nello

（中文字幕：爱你的
内罗）

Departure Bell

A chime rings out — a steam-era station bell announcing the train’s departure.

Change in Expression

“I” look at the letter, then look again at the marigold.

The sorrow has not fully disappeared from the face, but in the eyes, a new firmness begins to emerge.
