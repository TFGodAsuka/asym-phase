Suggested content: meadow opening shot, Nello montage, letter reveal, first fireflies.

Opening Shot

The camera slowly tilts down from the sky into a grand wide shot.

Clear sky. Drifting clouds. Wind sweeps across the meadow in waves. Snowy mountains stand in the distance. The sheep appear like scattered white dots.

Ambient sound: wind, distant sheep bells, the soft rustling of grass.

Voice-over (V.O.)

My world was small, bounded by the meadow, the Snowy mountain, and my grandmother's stories.

（中文字幕：在“我”有记忆起，“我”就和祖母一起生活。）

Montage Begins

  • Medium shot: a pair of elderly hands knitting, with the young “me” beside them.
  • Close-up: Nello the sheepdog nudges “my” hand with his wet nose.
  • Wide shot: little “me” stands alone in the open meadow, looking toward the far valley.

Voice-over (V.O.)

The only companion my age was Nello, the sheepdog. Then, one night, he was gone. Protecting us from the wolves.

（中文字幕：平日里只有牧羊犬内罗陪伴着“我”。第一次出远门，是去离牧羊小屋隔了一座山谷的小镇火车站。那时，陪伴“我”长大的内罗为了驱逐狼群再也没有回来。）

Subjective Shot

From the cottage window, “I” stare fearfully into the darkness outside. A wolf’s howl rises in the night.

Voice-over (V.O.)

After that, the world outside became a monster. I hid in the cottage, my courage buried with him.

Letter Motif

  • A letter is pushed through the gap beneath the door.
  • There is no address on the envelope, only a paw print.
  • Close-up: “my” trembling hand opens the letter.
  • The note reads:

“On the night the fireflies burn brightest, make a wish. The first train at dawn will bring it to you.”

（中文字幕：只要在萤火虫最璀璨的夜晚许下愿，清晨第一趟火车便会从远方带来你的愿望。）

Ending Image of Scene 01

Medium shot: “I” look out the window. A few ordinary fireflies drift in the air. But in “my” eyes, their glow gradually becomes unnaturally brilliant.

This signals that “I” have found a possible way to bring Nello back, conveyed through sound and light treatment rather than explicit dialogue.
