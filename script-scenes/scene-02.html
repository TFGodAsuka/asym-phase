Night / Forest and Stream / Exterior

Opening Movement / Voice-over (V.O.)

The shot tilts from the night sky down into the valley, landing on a wide view.

Along the way, I need to pass through meadows where sheep usually graze, woods in the valleys, and small streams. The gnarled and clawing trees, under the moonlight, emit a faint and eerie aura. (Wolf howling) The stream in the forest is the farthest place I have reached during the day.

（中文字幕：一路上，“我”需要经过平日里牧羊的草甸，山谷的树林和小溪。
张牙舞爪的树林在月光的映衬下，释放出幽幽的阴森气息。（狼的嚎叫）
森林中的小溪是“我”白天到过最遥远的地方。）

Crossing Point

Fixed wide shot. The moon hangs over the stream. Water moves quietly. A series of stepping stones and a broken log form the only way across.

The montage slows down here and the moment begins to be observed in detail. Hesitation becomes visible.

Action Build-up

  • “I” carefully hop across the first few stones.
  • At the center of the stream, “I” stop.
  • The next two stones are too far apart.
  • “I” take a deep breath and prepare to jump.

False Threat

At the exact moment of the jump, two dim green points flash in the trees on the opposite bank.

Panic breaks the movement. The jump deforms. “I” lose balance in midair.

Lantern Arc

Close-up: the backpack slips free.

The kerosene lamp tumbles through the air, drawing a luminous arc as it spins through the night, before falling into the stream with a soft extinguishing sound.

The light goes out.

Realization

“I” barely manage to land and stand upright, heart pounding. Looking again, the supposed “eyes” in the woods are only two moss-covered stones reflecting moonlight.

Panic collapses into exhaustion. “I” nearly cry, then turn back to watch the backpack drift away downstream, face full of despair.

Retreat and Threshold

Disheartened, “I” turn around and prepare to go back.

The shot lingers on the dark path that still lies beyond the stream, the darkness of the opposite bank waiting ahead.

First Summons of Hope

Just as “I” am about to leave the frame, a single firefly passes quietly in front of the camera.

Then a second. Then a third. Along the edge of the forest, countless small lights begin to appear.

This is not comfort. It is the first summons of hope.

Deceptive Cut

Wide shot returns to the shepherd’s cottage: night, bright moon, alpine meadow, distant snowy mountain, sheep gathered near the pen, the small house standing alone.

The room is empty. “I” am not inside. One of the sheep suddenly lets out a cry.

The audience is briefly led to believe that “I” still did not continue. Then the sound cuts into footsteps splashing across puddles.
