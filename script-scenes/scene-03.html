Jump Cut

Hard cut into motion: “I” keep moving through the darkness.

The body stumbles, falls, catches at branches, and pushes forward again. By now, the face is already streaked with mud and scraped by the journey.

Rapid Montage

  • Close-up: a foot sinks into a patch of mud.
  • Hands and shoulders force through dense thorn branches.
  • Breathing turns rough and uneven in the dark.
  • The path feels longer than the body can bear, but it does not stop.

Valley Pause

Exhausted, “I” lean against a rock in the valley and struggle to catch breath.

The camera slowly tilts upward toward the sky. Crickets and other night sounds fill the air.

Light in the Background

Through all of these images, the fireflies remain visible in the background, appearing and fading, never fully gone.

Their light hovers at the edge of the frame like a distant beacon, less like comfort than a silent guidance that keeps the movement alive.
