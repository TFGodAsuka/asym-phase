Suggested content: dawn station wide shot, child under pillar, tear drop, marigold reveal, paw-marked envelope.

Arrival

Grand wide shot: dawn stains the horizon red. A steam train, breathing white vapor, slowly enters the small station at the foot of the mountain.

Child at the Platform

Medium shot: little “me,” filthy and worn from the journey, curls up beneath a station pillar, face buried in folded knees. The shoulders tremble slightly.

Ambient sound: crowded voices, steam hissing, footsteps moving through the station.

Voice-over (V.O.)

I was so close. But sitting there, a sadness I'd never known washed over me. I knew, then. I knew I would never see Nello again.

（中文字幕：快要见到信中能实现愿望的火车，“我”年幼的心中却在此时泛起一阵前所未有的心酸， 几乎就要哭了出来。不知为什么，“我”真的感到我再也不能见到 Nello 了。）

Tear

Close-up: a tear falls to the station ground.

Shadow and Fragrance

A long shadow covers “me.” It pauses for a brief moment, then shifts away.

Close-up: “my” nose moves slightly, catching a faint trace of floral fragrance.

Reveal

“I” look up through blurred tears.

Focus settles: before “me” lies a complete marigold, and beside it, a familiar envelope marked with a paw print.

Silence Through Focus

As the tear-blurred image comes into focus, the surrounding station noise gradually quiets.

The world contracts into a single threshold image: the child, the flower, and the letter that should not already be here.
